New to FLO in 2017: Online Video Platform

What can I do?

You can upload and embed video and audio in any text edit box in FLO using our new online video plaform (Kaltura) and it will play on all devices.

All common, non-proprietary files are accepted, for example: mp4, mp3, wav, avi, mpeg, mov, flv.

Students: Getting Started

  1. Upload your file to My Media on FLO (above the Log Out menu option).
  2. From your FLO Topic site, embed it in a text edit box — such as a forum or assignment.
    • Note: media not added to a Topic site will be deleted after 6 months.

Teachers: Getting Started

  1. Upload you file to your My Media. It is private until you publish it.
  2. Publish it to the Media Vault for your FLO topic site.
  3. Embed media in your FLO site. Use any text edit box — such as a label.
